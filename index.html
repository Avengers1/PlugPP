Features

  • Auto-Woot

    Show your appreciation even though you're currently working on different things.

  • Auto-Join

    The waitlist is way too full again? No problem. Let the extension do it for you!

  • Service Notifications

    Let the extension display you even more information about the room in plug's built-in notification system.

  • Desktop Notifications

    Get notified about the important things. Mentions, the next track, your turn ... You decide!

  • Moderation Utilities

    Moderate your room with style and fix problems before they occur!
